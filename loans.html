Responsible Borrowing Tips

  • Always check the annual percentage rate (APR) and fees.
  • Borrow only what you can repay on time.
  • Compare multiple lenders before making decisions.
  • Read all loan agreements and disclosures carefully.
  • Maintain a healthy credit score to access better terms.
